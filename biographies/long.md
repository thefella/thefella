# Conor MacNeill Long Bio (~230 Words)

[Conor MacNeill] is an award-winning, fine-art landscape and destination photographer based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. He has over a decade of experience travelling the globe capturing emotive images of some of the world's most epic scenes. He has travelled to around 90 countries.

His clients include Adobe, Nikon, Lonely Planet, Instagram, Ford, BMW, The Independent, and the BBC. He has also worked for many tourism boards and travel companies, including Bhutan Tourism, Germany Tourism, Hawai'i Tourism, Visit Faroe Islands, Travel Alberta, and Uzbekistan Tourism.

He was also named [Photographer of The Year] 2026 at the Travel Media Awards and was shortlisted for Astronomy Photographer of the Year.

Conor also loves to teach photography. He founded [f/8 Workshops] where he runs landscape photography workshops around the world. He can also arrange bespoke workshops for individuals, small groups, and businesses. If you're unable to make these, Conor gives online 1-on-1 lessons to help you improve your photography.

Conor enjoys speaking at conferences, whether this is about photography, travel, or social media. He has also taught photography workshops at conferences and retreats.

He is an ambassador for [f-stop Gear], [SmugMug] / [Flickr], and [Steller Stories].

In his spare time, Conor likes to play the drums, hit the gym, [review movies], and [reads] Japanese books with cats on the covers. He is trained in several martial arts including kickboxing, BJJ, MMA, and is an [Urban Krav Maga] instructor.
