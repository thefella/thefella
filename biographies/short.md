# Conor MacNeill Short Bio (~50 Words)

[Conor MacNeill] is an award-winning, fine-art destination photographer who specialises in landscapes, cityscapes and astrophotography. He also runs [f/8 Workshops] where teaches photography workshops around the world. He also speaks at conferences, and works with many clients including tourism boards and automotive brands.
