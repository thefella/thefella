# Conor MacNeill Medium Bio (~100 Words)

[Conor MacNeill] is an award-winning, fine-art landscape and destination photographer based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. He travels the globe capturing emotive images of some of the world's most epic scenes.

His clients include tourism boards, travel companies, airlines, and automotive brands.

Conor also loves to teach photography. He runs [f/8 Workshops] where teaches photography workshops around the world. He can also arrange bespoke workshops for individuals, small groups, and businesses. If you're unable to make these, Conor gives online 1-on-1 lessons to help you improve your photography.

Conor enjoys speaking at conferences, whether this is about photography, travel, or social media.
